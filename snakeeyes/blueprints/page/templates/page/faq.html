Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about the game.

  • What is the objective of the game?

    The objective is to guess the outcome of a dice roll and win points.

  • How do I place a bet?

    You can place a bet by selecting your guess and confirming your wager.

  • What happens if I win?

    If you win, you will receive coins based on your bet and the odds.

  • How is the leaderboard calculated?

    The leaderboard is based on the total points earned from successful bets.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, you can invite friends to join and compete against each other.

  • Is there a mobile version of the game?

    Yes, the game is fully responsive and can be played on mobile devices.

  • How do I contact support?

    You can contact support via the contact form on our website or email us directly.
